Mr. Section

Mr. section is a creature with multiple different sections in his body.

For example: one arm could be incredibly muscular

and buff, while the other could be skinny

and wobbly. Or one eye could be bright blue

and bloodshot, while the other could be dark green

and hairy. This creature was inspired by

the song Section by LYNY.
